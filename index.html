The Aurèle Standard

Every piece,
an obsession

01

Months, Not Weeks

Each piece enters design no less than eight months before it is offered. Prototypes are made and destroyed until nothing remains to improve.

02

One Artisan, One Piece

A single craftsperson completes each bag from cutting to finishing. Their name is inscribed inside. Their work is their signature.

03

No Waitlists for Profit

We do not inflate scarcity. Three pieces exist because three is the precise number we can make without compromise. Period.

04

Lifetime Relationship

Every piece is restored, re-lined, re-patinated free of charge for the life of the original purchaser. You do not buy an object. You begin an agreement.
