The Private
Client Programme

A small number of clients are invited into a closer relationship with Aurèle. First access. Direct correspondence. An annual review with Céleste. And the knowledge that a piece is being made with you in mind.

40
What It Means
Programme Benefits

Not membership.
Relationship.

First Access

Private clients receive notification of new quarterly pieces fourteen days before public announcement. During this window, pieces may be reserved privately — no public competition.

Annual Conversation

Once a year, Céleste personally contacts each private client to understand how their pieces are wearing, what their life now requires, and whether a commission might be worth exploring.

Commission Priority

All four annual bespoke commission slots are offered to private clients before anyone else. If declined, they are opened to the public waitlist. Private clients have taken all four slots in every year since 2018.

Atelier Visits

Private clients are welcome in the atelier on Via dei Serragli by appointment. You may observe work in progress, discuss materials with the artisans, and — if a commission is underway — be present for any stage of its making.

Lifetime Restoration

All clients receive lifetime restoration for any piece purchased. Private clients additionally receive an annual conditioning service — your pieces are collected, treated, and returned at no charge.

Direct Correspondence

No ticketing systems, no customer service queues. Private clients correspond directly with Céleste or, for atelier matters, with Marco. A mobile number is provided. It is answered.
